Private Figures, Public Impressions

An ongoing project capturing private individuals with the depth usually reserved for public figures. By highlighting their unique stories, I aim to reveal the significance of their lives while keeping them in 'low exposure'.

Passion in Motion

A testament to my long-standing passion for photography. It showcases how I first explored my creative vision through the lens of a professional camera. I wanted to future-proof this memory and found a way to capture my subject's passion through my eyes, ultimately telling his story while reflecting my own.

Unveiling Urban Wonders

This project transforms stock images of man-made objects into captivating stories. By framing these everyday items in their natural settings, the project reveals the intricate details often overlooked, turning the familiar into a fresh perspective on the beauty of our built environment.

Archieves

Moments of success, determination, and growth, freezing time to reflect the journey towards excellence.
